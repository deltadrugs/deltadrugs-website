Your health information is protected under HIPAA. This notice describes how medical information about you may be used and disclosed.

Our Commitment to Your Privacy

Delta Drugs is required by law to maintain the privacy of your protected health information (PHI) and to provide you with this Notice of Privacy Practices. We are committed to safeguarding your PHI in compliance with the Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act (HIPAA) and applicable California law.

How We May Use and Disclose Your PHI

We may use and disclose your PHI for the following purposes without your written authorization:

  • Treatment — To provide, coordinate, or manage your healthcare and related services, including coordination with physicians, hospitals, and other providers.
  • Payment — To obtain payment for services, including billing your insurance plan, Medicare, or Medi-Cal.
  • Healthcare Operations — For quality assessment, training, licensing, and business activities necessary to operate our pharmacy.

Other Permitted Uses and Disclosures

  • As required by law (court orders, public health reporting, abuse reporting)
  • To prevent a serious threat to health or safety
  • For workers' compensation as authorized by law
  • To business associates who perform services on our behalf under HIPAA-compliant agreements

Uses Requiring Your Authorization

We will obtain your written authorization before using or disclosing your PHI for purposes not described in this notice, including most marketing communications and sale of PHI.

Your Rights Regarding Your PHI

  • Right to Access — Request copies of your health records
  • Right to Amend — Request corrections to your records
  • Right to an Accounting — Request a list of certain disclosures
  • Right to Request Restrictions — Ask us to limit uses or disclosures
  • Right to Confidential Communications — Request alternate contact methods
  • Right to a Paper Copy — Obtain a copy of this notice at any time
  • Right to Complain — File a complaint with us or the U.S. Department of Health and Human Services

Changes to This Notice

We reserve the right to change this notice. The revised notice will apply to all PHI we maintain. Current notices are available on our website and at our pharmacy location.
